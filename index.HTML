What is AI

This website provides information about Artificial Intelligence (AI), its applications, advantages, challenges, and future impact on society. Explore how AI is transforming the world and becoming an important part of our daily lives. From healthcare and education to business, transportation, and entertainment, AI is helping people work faster, solve problems more efficiently, and create innovative solutions. AI-powered technologies are improving productivity, reducing human effort, and making systems smarter and more reliable. This website aims to provide a clear understanding of AI, its importance in modern life, and its growing role in shaping the future. As technology continues to advance, Artificial Intelligence will become even more influential in improving industries, supporting innovation, and creating a smarter and more connected world.
